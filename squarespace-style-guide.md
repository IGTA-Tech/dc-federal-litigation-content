# DC Federal Litigation Counsel - Squarespace Style Guide

## Brand Identity

**Firm Name:** DC Federal Litigation Counsel
**Domain:** dcfederallitigation.com
**Tagline:** Federal Court Representation from Washington, DC

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors

**Navy Blue (Primary)**
- Hex: #1B365D
- RGB: 27, 54, 93
- Use: Headers, navigation, buttons, footer

**Gold Accent**
- Hex: #C5A572
- RGB: 197, 165, 114
- Use: Accents, highlights, icons, hover states

### Secondary Colors

**White**
- Hex: #FFFFFF
- Use: Background, text on dark backgrounds

**Light Gray**
- Hex: #F5F5F5
- Use: Section backgrounds, alternating sections

**Dark Gray (Text)**
- Hex: #333333
- Use: Body text, paragraphs

---

## Typography

### Headings

**Font Family:** Montserrat or similar sans-serif
**Weights:** 600 (semi-bold) for H1-H2, 500 (medium) for H3-H6

| Element | Size | Weight | Color |
|---------|------|--------|-------|
| H1 | 42px | 600 | Navy #1B365D |
| H2 | 32px | 600 | Navy #1B365D |
| H3 | 24px | 500 | Navy #1B365D |
| H4 | 20px | 500 | Navy #1B365D |

### Body Text

**Font Family:** Open Sans or similar sans-serif
**Size:** 16px
**Line Height:** 1.6
**Color:** Dark Gray #333333

### Navigation

**Font Family:** Montserrat
**Size:** 14px
**Weight:** 500
**Letter Spacing:** 0.5px
**Transform:** Uppercase

---

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Ascending bar chart icon (navy with gold accent on tallest bar)
- "DC FEDERAL LITIGATION" text below
- "COUNSEL" subtext with horizontal rule above

### Logo Placement
- Header: Left-aligned, max height 60px
- Footer: Centered, max height 80px
- Favicon: Bar chart icon only

### Logo Clear Space
- Minimum clear space equal to height of "C" in "COUNSEL"
- Never place text or other elements within clear space

## Squarespace Template Recommendations

### Recommended Templates
1. **Brine** - Clean, professional, highly customizable
2. **Bedford** - Modern, business-focused
3. **Montauk** - Minimalist, content-focused

### Template Settings

**Header:**
- Fixed navigation
- Transparent on hero, solid on scroll
- Logo left, navigation right
- Mobile: hamburger menu

**Footer:**
- Navy background (#1B365D)
- White text
- Three columns: Contact, Practice Areas, Quick Links
- Copyright bottom center

## Button Styles

### Primary Button
- Background: Navy #1B365D
- Text: White #FFFFFF
- Padding: 16px 32px
- Border Radius: 4px
- Hover: Gold #C5A572 background

### Secondary Button
- Background: Transparent
- Border: 2px solid Navy #1B365D
- Text: Navy #1B365D
- Hover: Navy background, white text

### CTA Button
- Background: Gold #C5A572
- Text: Navy #1B365D
- Weight: 600
- Hover: Darker gold shade

---

## Section Layouts

### Hero Section
- Full-width background image or solid navy
- Centered text, white on dark background
- H1 headline + subtext + CTA button
- Minimum height: 500px

### Service Cards
- White background with subtle shadow
- Navy icon at top (40px)
- H3 title
- Brief description
- "Learn More" link in gold

### Alternating Sections
- Alternate white (#FFFFFF) and light gray (#F5F5F5) backgrounds
- Keeps content visually organized
- Navy headings throughout

### FAQ Accordion
- Clean accordion style
- Navy question text
- Plus/minus icons in gold
- Smooth expand animation

---

## Image Guidelines

### Photography Style
- Professional, corporate imagery
- DC landmarks (Capitol, federal buildings) when appropriate
- Clean, well-lit
- Minimal or no text overlays

### Icons
- Simple line icons or solid icons
- Navy primary, gold for accents
- Consistent style throughout
- 40-60px for service icons

---

## Spacing

### Padding
- Section padding: 80px top/bottom
- Container max-width: 1200px
- Mobile section padding: 40px

### Margins
- Between sections: 0 (use padding)
- Between elements: 24px default
- Between paragraphs: 16px

## Mobile Responsiveness

### Breakpoints
- Desktop: 1200px+
- Tablet: 768px - 1199px
- Mobile: Below 768px

### Mobile Adjustments
- Stack columns vertically
- Reduce H1 to 32px
- Hamburger navigation
- Full-width buttons
- Reduce section padding to 40px

## SEO Elements

### Meta Titles
- Format: [Page Title] | DC Federal Litigation Counsel
- Max length: 60 characters

### Meta Descriptions
- Max length: 160 characters
- Include primary keyword
- Include call-to-action

### URL Structure
- Lowercase
- Hyphens between words
- No dates in URLs
- Example: /dc-local-counsel-pro-hac-vice
