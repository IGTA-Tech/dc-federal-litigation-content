*Admitted to the U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia, the U.S. Court of Federal Claims, and the U.S. Court of Appeals for the DC Circuit*

---

## When Administrative Remedies Aren't Enough

Most attorneys stop at administrative proceedings. When agencies deny your petition, delay your application indefinitely, or rule against your interests, many lawyers simply accept defeat.

**We don't.**

DC Federal Litigation PLLC takes cases to federal court when administrative channels fail. Our admission to the U.S. District Court for the District of Columbia and the U.S. Court of Federal Claims means we can pursue remedies that other attorneys cannot.

Whether you're challenging USCIS delays, protesting a lost contract, fighting EPA enforcement, or seeking monetary damages from the federal government, our federal court admission gives us the authority to advocate for you where it matters most.

## Our Practice Areas

### [Icon: Courthouse] Federal Court Services

**DC Local Counsel & Pro Hac Vice Sponsorship**
Out-of-state attorneys need a DC-barred sponsor to appear in DC federal courts. We provide pro hac vice sponsorship and local counsel services for the U.S. District Court for DC, Court of Federal Claims, and DC Circuit.

**Court of Federal Claims Litigation**
We represent clients in bid protests, contract disputes, and Tucker Act claims before the U.S. Court of Federal Claims, with appeals to the Federal Circuit.

**Federal Court Ad Hoc Counsel**
Limited scope representation for specific federal court appearances, oral arguments, and emergency filings.

---

### [Icon: Immigration] Immigration Federal Court Litigation

**USCIS Mandamus Lawsuits**
When USCIS delays your immigration application beyond reasonable timeframes, we file mandamus lawsuits in federal district court to force a decision.

**Visa Denial Appeals**
We challenge arbitrary visa denials through Administrative Procedure Act (APA) litigation in federal court.

---

### [Icon: Government Building] Government Contracts

**Bid Protests (GAO & COFC)**
We represent contractors challenging award decisions at both the Government Accountability Office and the Court of Federal Claims.

**SBA Certifications & Approvals**
Assistance with 8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, and WOSB certifications, including denial appeals.

**Suspension & Debarment Defense**
We defend contractors facing exclusion from federal contracting.

---

### [Icon: Scales] Environmental & Agency Litigation

**EPA Challenges**
We challenge EPA permit decisions, enforcement actions, and regulations in the DC Circuit and federal district court.

**FOIA Litigation**
When agencies refuse to release records, we sue in federal court to compel disclosure.

**Tucker Act Claims**
We pursue monetary damages against the federal government for contract breaches, takings, and statutory violations.
